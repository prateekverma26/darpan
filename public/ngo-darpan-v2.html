Four steps

From sign-up to unique ID

The whole process is online and typically completed in one sitting, ending with an e-signed affidavit.

Create an account

Register with your PAN, verify email and mobile by OTP, then set a password and 4-digit mPIN.

Add details & documents

Fill NPO details, members, working areas, past projects and activities. Upload the required proofs.

Review & e-sign

Check every section, add witness details, then e-sign the draft affidavit with an OTP to your chief functionary.

Receive your DARPAN ID

Your unique ID is generated instantly. Start applying for grants and exemptions across ministries.
